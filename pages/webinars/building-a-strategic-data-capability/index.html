SNOWPLOW WEBINAR

Building a strategic data capability

Lessons learned from building a robust data stack at VONQ

When it comes to building a robust and future-proof data stack, there are many important pieces to the puzzle. The data journey is complex and by no means an easy feat. That’s why learning from others who’ve already been through parts of their journey can empower you on your way.


In this webinar, we will look at some of the opportunities and challenges that can arise when evolving your data stack. We will draw on examples from recruitment marketing specialists VONQ, who use data to attract talent and advertise jobs on behalf of their customers.


In order to make better recommendations and provide actionable insights for recruiters, VONQ invested in a data warehouse and data model that fit their business needs. Natalia Pipas, Data Engineer at VONQ, will shed light on their data journey; their initial challenges, the lessons learned, what they’ve achieved and what’s next for the data team at VONQ.

Speakers

Natalia Pipas

Data Engineer at VONQ

Archit Goyal

Solutions Architecture Lead at Snowplow

In this discussion, our speakers will dive into:

  • The modern data stack landscape, looking at the different pieces needed derive actionable insights.
  • Best practices when investing in a data warehouse and data model that fit your business needs.
  • How VONQ approached their data journey to make better recommendations and provide actionable insights to their customers.
